WHO IS KOKO

Koko is no ordinary creature. He is a Bigfoot with a coconut for a head. Born deep in the tropical jungle, Koko wandered out of the forest one day, put on his blue tee and green shorts, laced up his sneakers, and never looked back.


Nobody knows exactly where he came from. Some say he fell from a coconut tree. Others say he is the coconut tree. What is certain: Koko is built different — part beast, part fruit, 100% legend.


He does not talk much. He just vibes. And somehow, the whole jungle follows.
